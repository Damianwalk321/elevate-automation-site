Refunds

Refunds are not guaranteed for partial subscription periods, unused time, or change-of-mind purchases. However, refunds may be considered in cases of duplicate charges, billing errors, or verified technical issues that materially prevented access to the paid service.

Cancellation

You may cancel your subscription at any time. Cancellation stops future billing but does not automatically create a refund for prior charges that have already been processed.

Trial Cancellations

If you cancel before the founder trial ends, future billing should stop. It is your responsibility to cancel before the billing date if you do not want the paid subscription to begin.

Charge Reviews

If you believe you were charged incorrectly, contact Elevate Automation promptly with the email used for your account and the relevant billing details so the charge can be reviewed.
