EXPECTATIONS

How beta testers should use the tool.

The beta is designed for controlled testing, product improvement, and measured rollout. Use it responsibly.

Use It Seriously

This is not passive access. The value of the beta comes from real testing, real usage, and real feedback.

Respect Limits

Posting limits and safety controls exist to protect users, the tool, and platform longevity. Do not try to abuse volume.

Communicate Fast

If something breaks, report it quickly. Fast communication during beta is one of the biggest advantages early testers bring.

Beta access may be limited, adjusted, or revoked if the tool is abused, shared improperly, or used outside intended beta conditions.
SUPPORT

Where to go if you hit friction.

Keep support centralized. That helps fixes happen faster and keeps beta cleaner for everyone.

Before Reporting an Issue

  • Confirm the latest extension build is installed
  • Restart the browser if needed
  • Note the exact field or workflow step that failed
  • Take a screenshot or short screen recording if possible

What to Include in a Bug Report

  • Your name and email
  • Vehicle or workflow being tested
  • What you expected to happen
  • What actually happened
  • Any screenshot or screen recording
